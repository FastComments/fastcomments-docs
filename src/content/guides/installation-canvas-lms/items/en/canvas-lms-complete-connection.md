#### Enter the Client ID in FastComments

Go back to <a href="https://fastcomments.com/auth/my-account/canvas-lti-config" target="_blank">My Account > Canvas LTI Config</a> in FastComments.

Find your configuration and paste the **Client ID** you copied from Canvas into the **Client ID** field.

If you haven't already, enter your **Platform URL** (your Canvas instance URL, e.g. `https://yourschool.instructure.com`).

Click **Save**.

#### Install the External App in Canvas

In Canvas, go to **Admin** > select your account > **Settings** > **Apps** tab.

Click **+ App** and configure:

1. Set **Configuration Type** to **By Client ID**.
2. Paste the **Client ID** from the Developer Keys table.
3. Click **Submit**.
4. Confirm the installation when prompted.

FastComments is now installed at the account level and available to all courses.
