If your FastComments account is hosted in the EU, update two settings at `Administration > Configuration > Content > FastComments`:

- **CDN URL** - `https://cdn-eu.fastcomments.com`
- **Site URL** - `https://eu.fastcomments.com`

You'll also want to pull your Tenant ID and API Secret from the EU dashboard at [eu.fastcomments.com/auth/my-account/api](https://eu.fastcomments.com/auth/my-account/api) instead of the default US dashboard. Everything else in the module works the same.
