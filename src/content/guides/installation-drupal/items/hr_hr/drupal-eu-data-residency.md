Ako je vaš FastComments račun hostan u EU, ažurirajte dvije postavke u `Administration > Configuration > Content > FastComments`:

- **CDN URL** - `https://cdn-eu.fastcomments.com`
- **Site URL** - `https://eu.fastcomments.com`

Također dohvatite svoj Tenant ID i API Secret s EU nadzorne ploče na [eu.fastcomments.com/auth/my-account/api](https://eu.fastcomments.com/auth/my-account/api) umjesto s zadane američke nadzorne ploče. Sve ostalo u modulu funkcionira isto.
