Уредићемо изглед нашег блога да додамо FastComments на све наше објаве на блогу.

In our Blog admin panel, we're going to click the `Post Layout` button.

<div class="screenshot white-bg">
    <div class="title">Отворите Post Layout</div>

Then we're going to add a `Raw HTML` block (drag it to where you want the comment area):

<div class="screenshot white-bg">
    <div class="title">Додајте Raw HTML блок</div>
    <img class="screenshot-image" src="/images/installation-guides/systeme-add-html-block.png" alt="Додајте Raw HTML блок" />
</div>

Now move your mouse over the block until it says Raw HTML and click it:

<div class="screenshot white-bg">
    <div class="title">Изаберите блок</div>
