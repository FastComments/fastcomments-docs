Θα επεξεργαστούμε τη διάταξη του blog μας για να προσθέσουμε το FastComments σε όλες τις αναρτήσεις του.

In our Blog admin panel, we're going to click the `Post Layout` button.

<div class="screenshot white-bg">
    <div class="title">Άνοιγμα Post Layout</div>
    <img class="screenshot-image" src="/images/installation-guides/systeme-edit-post-layout.png" alt="Άνοιγμα Post Layout" />
</div>

Then we're going to add a `Raw HTML` block (drag it to where you want the comment area):

<div class="screenshot white-bg">
    <div class="title">Προσθήκη Raw HTML Block</div>
    <img class="screenshot-image" src="/images/installation-guides/systeme-add-html-block.png" alt="Προσθήκη Raw HTML Block" />
</div>

Now move your mouse over the block until it says Raw HTML and click it:

<div class="screenshot white-bg">
    <div class="title">Επιλέξτε Block</div>
    <img class="screenshot-image" src="/images/installation-guides/systeme-edit-block.png" alt="Επιλέξτε Block" />
</div>

On the left hand side, now we'll have the option to click `Edit Code` - click that:
