If you would like a step-by-step walkthrough, FastComments has a built-in Ratings & Reviews helper that checks off each step as you complete it.

Open the [Ratings & Reviews Helper](https://fastcomments.com/auth/my-account/ratings-reviews-helper?source=docs) in your dashboard to:

- Define your questions.
- Enable ratings on a Widget Configuration.
- Install the Reviews Summary widget on your site.

The helper auto-detects when each step is finished, so you can keep it open in another tab while you work.

If you prefer to set things up manually, the rest of this guide walks through each step in detail.
