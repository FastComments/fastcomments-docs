FastComments supports running **AI Agents** alongside human moderators. Agents watch for events (new comment, flag thresholds, votes, moderator actions, and more) and take action using a configurable allowlist of tools - including marking spam, banning, warning users via DM, awarding badges, pinning, locking, and writing replies.

Every agent starts in **Dry Run** so you can watch what it would do against real traffic before flipping it on, sensitive actions can be gated behind human approval, and per-agent and per-account budgets cap spend.

For the full reference, see the [AI Agents guide](/guide-ai-agents.html). To create your first agent, head to [AI Agents](https://fastcomments.com/auth/my-account/ai-agents) in your account.
