Da bismo pravilno dodali FastComments u Ghost, moramo izmeniti fajlove teme.

Dakle, počnimo tako što ćemo preuzeti vašu postojeću ili željenu temu.

Idite na kontrolnu tablu vašeg sajta i kliknite na ikonicu zupčanika u donjem levom uglu:

<div class="screenshot white-bg">
    <div class="title">Otvori podešavanja</div>
    <img class="screenshot-image" src="/images/installation-guides/ghost-step-1-1-open-settings.png" alt="Otvori podešavanja" />
</div>

Zatim izaberite opciju `Design`:

<div class="screenshot white-bg">
    <div class="title">Izaberite `Design`</div>
    <img class="screenshot-image" src="/images/installation-guides/ghost-step-1-2-select-design.png" alt="Izaberite dizajn" />
</div>

Iako tehnički ne menjamo temu, kliknite na `Change Theme` u donjem levom uglu:

<div class="screenshot white-bg">
    <div class="title">Promeni temu</div>
    <img class="screenshot-image" src="/images/installation-guides/ghost-step-1-3-change-theme.png" alt="Promeni temu" />
</div>

U gornjem desnom uglu izaberite `Advanced`:

<div class="screenshot white-bg">
    <div class="title">Izaberite `Advanced`</div>
    <img class="screenshot-image" src="/images/installation-guides/ghost-step-1-4-select-advanced.png" alt="Izaberite `Advanced`" />
</div>

...a zatim preuzmite vašu trenutnu ili željenu temu:

<div class="screenshot white-bg">
    <div class="title">Preuzmite temu</div>
    <img class="screenshot-image" src="/images/installation-guides/ghost-step-1-5-download.png" alt="Preuzmite temu" />
</div>
