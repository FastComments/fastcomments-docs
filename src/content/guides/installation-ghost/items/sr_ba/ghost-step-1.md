Da bismo pravilno dodali FastComments u Ghost, moramo urediti datoteke teme.

Dakle, počnimo s preuzimanjem vaše postojeće ili željene teme.

Idite na nadzornu ploču vaše stranice i kliknite zupčanik u donjem lijevom uglu:

<div class="screenshot white-bg">
    <div class="title">Otvorite Postavke</div>
    <img class="screenshot-image" src="/images/installation-guides/ghost-step-1-1-open-settings.png" alt="Otvorite Postavke" />
</div>

Zatim odaberite opciju `Design`:

<div class="screenshot white-bg">
    <div class="title">Odaberite Dizajn</div>
    <img class="screenshot-image" src="/images/installation-guides/ghost-step-1-2-select-design.png" alt="Odaberite Dizajn" />
</div>

Iako tehnički ne mijenjamo temu, kliknite na `Change Theme` u donjem lijevom uglu:

<div class="screenshot white-bg">
    <div class="title">Promijeni Temu</div>
    <img class="screenshot-image" src="/images/installation-guides/ghost-step-1-3-change-theme.png" alt="Promijeni Temu" />
</div>

U gornjem desnom uglu odaberite `Advanced`:

<div class="screenshot white-bg">
    <div class="title">Odaberite Napredno</div>
    <img class="screenshot-image" src="/images/installation-guides/ghost-step-1-4-select-advanced.png" alt="Odaberite Napredno" />
</div>

...a zatim preuzmite svoju trenutnu ili željenu temu:

    <div class="title">Preuzmite Temu</div>
