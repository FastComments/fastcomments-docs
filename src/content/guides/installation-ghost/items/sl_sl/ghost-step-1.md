Da pravilno dodate FastComments v Ghost, moramo urediti datoteke teme.

Torej začnimo s prenosom vaše obstoječe ali želene teme.

Pojdite na nadzorno ploščo vaše strani in kliknite ikono zobnika v spodnjem levem kotu:

<div class="screenshot white-bg">
    <div class="title">Odprite nastavitve</div>
    <img class="screenshot-image" src="/images/installation-guides/ghost-step-1-1-open-settings.png" alt="Odprite nastavitve" />
</div>

Nato izberite možnost `Design`:

<div class="screenshot white-bg">
    <div class="title">Izberite Design</div>
    <img class="screenshot-image" src="/images/installation-guides/ghost-step-1-2-select-design.png" alt="Izberite Design" />
</div>

Čeprav tehnično ne spreminjamo teme, kliknite `Change Theme` v spodnjem levem kotu:

<div class="screenshot white-bg">
    <div class="title">Spremenite temo</div>
    <img class="screenshot-image" src="/images/installation-guides/ghost-step-1-3-change-theme.png" alt="Spremenite temo" />
</div>

V zgornjem desnem kotu izberite `Advanced`:

<div class="screenshot white-bg">
    <div class="title">Izberite Advanced</div>
    <img class="screenshot-image" src="/images/installation-guides/ghost-step-1-4-select-advanced.png" alt="Izberite Advanced" />
</div>

...in nato prenesite svojo trenutno ali želeno temo:
